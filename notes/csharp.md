# C\#

- Repeat String: `new string('a', 12)`


## DateTime Formatting

```
d = Day of month 1-31
dd = Day of month 01-31
ddd = Mon
dddd = Monday
h = Hour, 1 to 12
hh = Hour, 01 to 12
H = Hour, 0 to 23
HH = Hour, 00 to 23
m = minute, 0 to 59
mm = minute, 00 to 60
M = month, 1 to 12
MM = month, 01 to 12
tt = AM/PM
```

## Numeric Formatting

```
F{num} : Fixed point, with number of digits.
```
